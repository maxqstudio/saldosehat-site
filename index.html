About SaldoSehat

Clear records for daily personal finance

SaldoSehat is built to help users record and review personal cash flow with a simple, local data experiences. It is not a bank, loan provider, paylater service, credit scoring service, or official financial advisor

Daily records

Record personal income and expenses with dates, categories, wallets, and notes so your cash flow is easier to review

Wallets

Separate balances by cash, bank accounts, e-wallets, or e-money cards. E-money cards mean prepaid balances, not credit cards or paylater

Target

Create savings targets or budget plans and track progress from time to time

Loans & receivables

Record money you need to pay or receive, including notes, status, and due dates when needed

Export & backup

Create personal export and backup files anytime you want and anyplace you want to save it

Ask

Use chat-style help to read summaries from the data available in the app. Ask is not official financial advice use it as first help to understand your financial condition

Privacy-first basics

Your main data stays on your device

Main app data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for protecting your device, app access, export files, and backup files after they are created
