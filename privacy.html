Privacy Policy

SaldoSehat helps users record and review personal finances, including income, expenses, wallets, targets, loans, and receivables

Main SaldoSehat data is stored on your device. Protecting your device, app access, export files, and backup files remains your responsibility. Reminder notifications can be made more private to help protect sensitive information

1. Data you enter

SaldoSehat may store data that you enter into the app, such as transactions, wallet names, targets, loan notes, receivable notes, categories, due dates, and other notes that you create yourself

2. Local data storage

Main app data is stored locally on your device. If the app is removed, app data is cleared, or the device is damaged, data may be lost if you have not created an export or backup

3. Export and backup

Export and backup features are provided to help you keep personal records. SaldoSehat backup files use the .saldosehat format. After an export or backup file is created, the security of that file becomes your responsibility. Store it in a safe place and do not share it with parties you do not trust

4. Ads and third-party services

The free version of SaldoSehat may show ads. Advertising services or other third-party services used by the app may process certain information according to their own policies. Technical details may change as the app and services evolve

5. Notifications

SaldoSehat may use local notifications for reminders, such as activity reminders, due date reminders, and savings target reminders. You can make notification content more private through the app setting, or manage notification permission through your device settings

When notification details are hidden, SaldoSehat uses generic reminder copy. When details are not hidden, reminders may be more informative but should not show money amounts in the notification title or body

6. Ask

Ask provides help based on templates and data available in the app. It is not an official financial advisor and does not replace consultation with a qualified professional

7. Security

We aim to keep the app simple and safe to use. However, device security, app access, backup files, export files, and any shared screenshots remain the responsibility of the user

8. Policy changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect feature changes, operational needs, security needs, or platform policy requirements

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, use the Support page or contact us by email
